### [Odysseus Returns to Ithaca](https://x.com/i/status/2032411369786531841)
*2026-03-13* | Tags: odyssey, homer, patience, strategy, perseverance, greek-literature

The homecoming scene from Homer's Odyssey where Odysseus returns to find his palace occupied by suitors. Rather than attacking immediately, he disguises himself as a beggar, endures their insults, and studies their habits—demonstrating strategic patience until the moment is right.

**Key Points:**
- Strategic patience: delaying action until the moment is right
- The real danger was not the journey but someone taking his place
- A man's home is not truly his unless he is willing to fight for it
